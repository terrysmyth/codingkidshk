Online Quizes

The online quizes are a helpful tool to help in the learning process whilst also finding holes in your knowledge. The main reason for the quizes is to access your progress toward taking the W3Schools certificates in programming. These online certifications will be the beginning of your career as a coder and will open all kinds of doors. Click here to find out more about these certificates!

Online Sandbox

This basic HTML/CSS sandbox allows students to try out new and exciting code instantly. No setup required and no programes need be downloaded. You can save your examples, and also the sandbox can be used to submit home assignments for particular classes.

Online Videos

While still in progress, these online videos are specifically made for those young coders out there (10-16yrs). The videos are short and concise to provide bite-sized lessons on particular subjects. These videos will be great for parents to sit and watch with your child at home before tyring the skills taught in the online sandbox!
