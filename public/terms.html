Terms & Conditions

1. Fees

Class fees are payable by the term and are due on or before the first class of the session. In the case where class dates fall on a public/statutory holiday, term fees will be calculated on a pro rata basis. Payment can be made by bank transfer or cheque and is non-refundable. All cheques are made payable to 'Terence Wayburne'. 

Please email info@tmrwdigital.com for bank transfer details. Fees will not be waived if your child is unable to attend for any reason, including illness or vacation. Late enrolments in the term will be accepted upon the director’s discretion, and fees will be pro-rated accordingly.

2. Health & Safety

In order to maintain appropriate standards of Health & Safety, parents/guardians are responsible for accurately disclosing any prior medical or physical conditions on this form, updating any conditions as they arise, and to notify the teacher prior to the commencement of class. If you are in any doubt as to the suitability of the class for your child, please consult your doctor. By submitting this form you expressly acknowledge the engagement in class under the care and supervision of Coding Kids Hong Kong and it’s instructors in the course of tuition.

3. Adverse Weather & Class Cancellation

All classes will be automatically canceled for Typhoon Signal 8, Black Rain Warning or higher. Refunds due to the weather or school closure are not possible.

Coding Kids Hong Kong reserves the right to cancel classes due to instructor’s health condition. If a class is cancelled for this reason, parents will be notified as soon as possible, and fees for this session will be refunded, or a makeup class arranged.

4. Instructor Substitution

Coding Kids Hong Kong reserves the right to substitute instructors as and when it is necessary. No refunds will be given in this case.

5. Exclusions

If a student is persistently disruptive, and/or the teacher assesses that their participation negatively affects others in the class, the teacher may choose to exclude the student to maintain a harmonious atmosphere. No refund will be made for a day missed or cost of transport.

In the interests of health & safety, students who attend class unprepared or improperly attired may be asked to sit out the class. No refund will be given in this case.

6. Withdrawals

No refunds will be given for withdrawals or cancellations made after 1 month of session start date. Please notify Coding Kids Hong Kong in writing at least 1 month prior to the withdrawal date if you wish to withdraw your child from our school.

7. Liability

Coding Kids Hong Kong does not accept liability for personal injury or death of any participant unless directly caused by the proven negligence by the company or it servants.

8. Photography, Videography & Media

Should Coding Kids Hong Kong require the use of media (such as, but not limited to, photos and videos) from classes, events, performances etc. the parent/guardian will be provided with an additional release form to permit or deny the use of the aforementioned media.
